## Overview

## Key Definitions

### Active Seat
A seat currently assigned to an employee who is actively enrolled in or completing training. Active seats count toward your tier's maximum limit.

### Archived Seat
A seat previously held by an employee who has either:
- Completed their training and received certification
- Been rotated out (terminated, transferred, etc.)

Archived seats do NOT count toward your active seat limit, allowing you to reassign that capacity to new employees.

### Rotational Buffer
The number of employees you can archive/rotate through within your subscription period before requiring a tier upgrade. This provides flexibility for organizations with natural staff turnover.

### Subscription Period
12 months from the date of purchase or renewal.

## Seat Rotation Policy

### How Rotation Works

1. **Assign**: Add an employee to an active seat to begin their training
2. **Train**: Employee completes the 24-module RVT curriculum
3. **Complete/Archive**: Upon completion (or employee departure), seat status changes to "archived"
4. **Reassign**: Archived seat frees capacity for a new employee

### Rotation Rules

- Organizations may freely rotate employees within their tier limits
- Total **active seats** at any moment must not exceed the tier's `max_active_seats`
- Total **archived seats** must not exceed the tier's `rotational_buffer`
- Completed employees retain their certification regardless of seat status
- Archived employees can be reactivated if within rotational buffer limits

### Example Scenario

A **Starter** tier organization (35 active seats, 15 rotational buffer):
- Can have up to 35 employees actively training at once
- Can rotate up to 15 additional employees through the year
- Total employees served per year: up to 50

## Upgrading & Downgrading

### Upgrades
- Available at any time during subscription
- Prorated pricing based on remaining subscription period
- Immediate access to additional seat capacity
- New tier limits apply instantly

### Downgrades
- Processed at renewal only
- Must reduce active seats to fit new tier limits before downgrade
- Archived seats beyond new buffer will be purged

## Terms & Conditions

### Seat Usage
- Seats are non-transferable between organizations
- One seat = one unique employee
- Duplicate accounts are not permitted
- Sharing login credentials is prohibited

### Data Retention
- Completed employee records retained for 7 years per MCA requirements
- Archived employees retain access to their certificates
- Training history and progress data retained for audit purposes

### Termination
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice
- No refunds for unused subscription period
- Employee certificates remain valid regardless of subscription status
