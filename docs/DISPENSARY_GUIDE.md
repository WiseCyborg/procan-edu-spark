# Dispensary Operations Guide

## 🎯 Overview

This guide provides comprehensive instructions for dispensary managers and staff on using the Cannabis Education Platform for compliance training and certification management.

## 🏢 For Dispensary Managers

### Getting Started

#### Initial Application Process
1. **Submit Application**: Complete the dispensary application form
   - Organization name and contact information
   - License number and address details
   - Estimated number of employees
   - Requested training credits

2. **Application Review**: Admin team reviews application (typically 1-3 business days)
   - License verification
   - Background checks
   - Compliance status review

3. **Payment Processing**: Upon approval, complete payment setup
   - PayPal payment processing
   - Training credit allocation
   - Organization activation

4. **Access Key Delivery**: Receive unique dispensary access key
   - Share with employees for registration
   - Keep secure and confidential
   - Contact support for replacement if lost

### Dispensary Manager Portal

#### Dashboard Overview
Access your management portal at `/dispensary` with your manager credentials:

- **Employee Overview**: Complete staff training status
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time completion monitoring
- **Compliance Metrics**: Organization compliance score
- **Certificate Management**: Active and expired certificates
- **Communication Tools**: Messaging and announcements

#### Key Features
- **Real-time Analytics**: Training completion rates and trends
- **Automated Alerts**: Notifications for incomplete training or expiring certificates
- **Compliance Reporting**: Export reports for regulatory requirements
- **Staff Management**: Invite, monitor, and manage employee training

### Staff Management

#### Onboarding New Employees

**Method 1: Access Key Registration**
1. Provide employee with dispensary access key
2. Employee registers at platform URL
3. Enter access key during registration
4. Complete profile setup
5. Begin training immediately

**Method 2: Staff Invitation System**
```
1. Access Dispensary Portal → Staff Management
2. Click "Invite New Employee"
3. Enter employee details:
   - Full name and email address
   - Role assignment (typically 'student')
   - Custom welcome message (optional)
4. Click "Send Invitation"
5. Employee receives email with registration link
6. Track invitation status in dashboard
```

#### Monitoring Employee Progress

**Individual Employee Tracking**
- **Progress Overview**: Module completion percentage (0-100%)
- **Time Tracking**: Hours spent in training
- **Quiz Performance**: Individual module quiz scores
- **Current Status**: Active module and completion timeline
- **Certificate Status**: Earned certificates and expiration dates

**Bulk Progress Monitoring**
```
1. Navigate to Dispensary Portal → Employee Progress
2. View organization-wide statistics:
   - Total employees enrolled
   - Completion percentage by module
   - Average training time
   - Upcoming certificate expirations
3. Filter and sort by:
   - Completion status
   - Department or role
   - Start date or deadline
   - Certificate expiration
```

#### Compliance Management

**Daily Compliance Tasks**
- Review employee progress reports
- Monitor training completion deadlines
- Check for expiring certificates
- Address incomplete training issues

**Weekly Compliance Review**
- Generate organization compliance report
- Review training completion trends
- Plan training schedule for new hires
- Update employee information as needed

**Monthly Compliance Reporting**
```
1. Access Dispensary Portal → Compliance Reports
2. Generate monthly compliance summary:
   - Training completion statistics
   - Certificate status report
   - Employee compliance scores

```

### Communication & Notifications

#### Automated Notifications
The system automatically sends notifications for:
- **Training Reminders**: Overdue module completion
- **Certificate Expiration**: 30, 15, and 7-day warnings
- **Compliance Alerts**: Organization compliance score drops
- **System Updates**: Platform updates and maintenance

#### Manual Communication
```
1. Navigate to Dispensary Portal → Communications
2. Create announcement or message:
   - Select recipients (all staff or specific employees)
   - Compose message with urgency level
   - Schedule delivery (immediate or future)
   - Track message delivery and read status
```

#### Emergency Communications
- **Immediate Alerts**: Critical compliance or system issues
- **Training Deadlines**: Urgent completion requirements
- **Regulatory Updates**: New training requirements or changes
- **System Maintenance**: Planned downtime notifications

## 👨‍💼 For Students/Employees

### Getting Started

#### Registration Process

**Using Dispensary Access Key**
1. Go to platform registration page
2. Enter your dispensary's unique access key
3. Complete profile information:
   - Full name and contact details
   - Date of birth and address
   - Emergency contact information
   - Job title and department
4. Verify email address
5. Set up optional multi-factor authentication
6. Begin training program

**Using Manager Invitation**
1. Check email for invitation from your manager
2. Click invitation link (valid for 7 days)
3. Complete registration process
4. Link automatically to your organization
5. Begin training immediately

#### Profile Setup Best Practices
- **Complete Information**: Ensure all required fields are filled
- **Accurate Details**: Verify contact information and job title
- **Security Settings**: Enable MFA for account protection
- **Communication Preferences**: Set notification preferences

### Training Program

#### Course Structure
**18-Module Training Program** covering:
1. Cannabis Law and Regulations
2. Product Knowledge and Safety
3. Customer Service and Sales Techniques
4. Inventory Management
5. Security and Loss Prevention
6. Quality Control and Testing
7. Medical Cannabis Considerations
8. Responsible Vendor Practices
9. Compliance Documentation
10. Emergency Procedures
11. Product Storage and Handling
12. Customer Education
13. Record Keeping Requirements
14. Advertising and Marketing Rules
15. Tax and Financial Compliance
16. Workplace Safety
17. Professional Development
18. Ethics and Professional Standards

#### Module Navigation
```
1. Access your dashboard at `/dashboard`
2. View course progress overview
3. Click on next available module
4. Complete module content:
   - Watch training video (if available)
   - Read module content
   - Take module quiz (80% passing score)
5. Proceed to next module upon completion
```

#### Quiz and Assessment Guidelines
- **Passing Score**: 80% minimum on all quizzes
- **Retake Policy**: Unlimited attempts with 24-hour cooldown
- **Time Limits**: No time restrictions on module quizzes
- **Final Exam**: Comprehensive exam after all modules complete
- **Proctoring**: Photo verification required for final exam

### Progress Tracking

#### Personal Dashboard Features
- **Progress Bar**: Visual completion percentage
- **Module Status**: Completed, in-progress, and upcoming modules
- **Time Tracking**: Total hours spent in training
- **Quiz Scores**: Individual module performance
- **Certificate Status**: Earned certificates and renewal dates

#### Tracking Your Progress
```
1. Log into your dashboard
2. Review current completion status
3. Identify next required module
4. Set personal completion timeline
5. Track quiz performance and improvement areas
```

### Final Exam and Certification

#### Final Exam Requirements
- **Eligibility**: Complete all 18 modules with passing scores
- **Format**: Comprehensive multiple-choice exam
- **Duration**: Maximum 2 hours
- **Passing Score**: 80% minimum
- **Retake Policy**: One retake after 48-hour waiting period

#### Photo Verification Process
```
1. Access final exam when eligible
2. Complete photo verification:
   - Take clear photo of yourself
   - Upload government-issued ID
   - Confirm identity verification
3. Begin exam with time tracking
4. Submit completed exam for scoring
```

#### Certificate Generation
Upon passing the final exam:
1. **Automatic Generation**: Certificate created immediately
2. **PDF Download**: Available in your dashboard
3. **Verification Number**: Unique certificate number assigned
4. **Expiration Date**: Certificate valid for 2 years
5. **Employer Notification**: Manager receives completion notification

### Ongoing Compliance

#### Certificate Management
- **Download**: Access PDF certificate anytime
- **Verification**: Provide certificate number for verification
- **Renewal**: Complete renewal training before expiration
- **Replacement**: Request new copy if lost

#### Continuing Education
- **Renewal Requirements**: Complete updated training every 2 years
- **Regulatory Updates**: Additional training for law changes
- **Professional Development**: Optional advanced training modules
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Regular knowledge assessments

## 🚨 Troubleshooting Common Issues

### For Managers

#### Employee Registration Issues
**Problem**: Employee can't register with access key
**Solutions**:
1. Verify access key is correct and not expired
2. Check organization payment status
3. Ensure employee email isn't already registered
4. Contact support for key reset if needed

**Problem**: Employee progress not showing
**Solutions**:
1. Confirm employee is linked to your organization
2. Check if employee completed profile setup
3. Verify training has been started
4. Refresh dashboard and clear browser cache

#### Compliance Reporting Issues
**Problem**: Compliance score seems incorrect
**Solutions**:
1. Verify all employee data is current
2. Check for completed but uncertified employees
3. Review expired certificate status
4. Contact support for score recalculation

### For Students

#### Login and Access Issues
**Problem**: Can't access course modules
**Solutions**:
1. Verify payment status with your manager
2. Ensure profile is completely filled out
3. Check if organization credits are available
4. Clear browser cache and try again

**Problem**: Quiz not accepting answers
**Solutions**:
1. Ensure stable internet connection
2. Disable browser extensions temporarily
3. Try different browser or device
4. Contact support if issue persists

#### Progress Tracking Issues
**Problem**: Completed modules showing as incomplete
**Solutions**:
1. Ensure quiz was passed with 80% or higher
2. Check if module content was fully viewed
3. Verify internet connection during completion
4. Contact support for progress correction
